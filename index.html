Blog One

There are many fun things to do in the St. Lawrence River region. Between Canada and the US there are many great sites to see. Some of the top favorite places to visit include Bolt Castle and Singer Castle, which can be visited on the Uncle Sams Boat Tours. Other things to do in the area are try some of the great local restaurants between Cape Vincent, Clayton and Alexandria Bay
